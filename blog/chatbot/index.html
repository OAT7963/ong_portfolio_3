Chatbot AI using Neural Networks - Education pathways in Malaysia

Chatbot AI using Neural Networks - Education pathways in Malaysia

Published on June 20, 2020 by Ong Aun Ter

Introduction and Project Motivation

Currently, there is no easily-accessible platform for high schoolers to obtain information about all the courses being offered across different universities in Malaysia. Typically, students will consult the course advisors of a specific university which would result in highly biased responses. I am building a neutral Education Pathway Advisor Chatbot AI using neural networks to overcome this issue.

Progress

My chatbot AI has already been built and I am currently working on feeding it as many pathway data as possible. An example of a conversation between the user and the bot would be:

Bot: Hi, welcome to (APP NAME)! Tell me what are your interests and I will shortlist a few university courses for you.

User: My interests are hard science, I think I’d like to major in physics.

Bot: Noted. These are the universities that are offering BSc Physics: University of Malaya, TAR UC, UTP.

Only the chatbot has been built, it took me about 2 days to build the chatbot using Neural Networks. I suspect the data will take me 4-6 months to complete. Then I will seek an app developer to develop this idea.
